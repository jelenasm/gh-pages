About me

Postdoctoral Researcher
Integrated Science Lab
Department of Physics
Umeå University, Sweden 

 

           Email: jelena.smiljanic@umu.se 

 

I was born in Serbia where I received my bachelor’s and master’s degree in telecommunications from the University of Belgrade. After finishing master studies, I started my research career at the Institute of Physics Belgrade. My research interests focus on applying statistical physics, complex networks theory and data science techniques, to study how different real-world systems are organized. I received my PhD from the University of Belgrade in 2017. In my dissertation, I have analyzed the interplay between network structure and the dynamics of event-driven social groups.

I joined Icelab as a postdoctoral researcher in August 2018 and started to work on dealing with uncertainties in community detection algorithms.
